Humidity

There is no direct correlation between humidity and latitude. We notice that it is more humid at the equator and as we approach towards the north pole compared to the south pole as per trend seen in plot (Latitude Vs Humidity), we can thus conclude that the higher the temperature the more humid it gets in that city. However, the scatterplot shows that most of the cities have humidity of 60% or higher.
